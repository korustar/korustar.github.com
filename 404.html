KoruStar

KoruStar Ltd. is a software development consultancy, started in January 2011. KoruStar is based in New Zealand, and mainly works for clients in Europe and the U.S.

Payment Industry

KoruStar's primary client is an international payment processor called SIX Payment Services.

SIX provide integrated payment solutions for merchants worldwide.
